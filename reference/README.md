# reference/ — context that makes AI edits sound like *you*

This folder is **private notes for the AI**, not part of your website. Nothing here is
published or visible to your visitors — it exists so that when you ask Claude to change your
site, it already knows your voice, your business, and your customers, and writes like you would.

**Fill in the files below.** The more you put here, the better your edits get. A few honest
sentences beat a blank file; you don't have to complete everything at once.

| File | What to put in it |
|------|-------------------|
| [`voice-and-tone.md`](voice-and-tone.md) | How you sound — words you love, words you avoid, examples |
| [`about-the-company.md`](about-the-company.md) | What you do, your story, what makes you different |
| [`customers-and-personas.md`](customers-and-personas.md) | Who buys from you, what they care about, how they talk |
| [`sales-and-marketing.md`](sales-and-marketing.md) | Key messages, offers, proof, taglines, links to assets |

You can also drop in extra files (a style guide, a brand PDF, past newsletters, FAQs) — Claude
will read the whole folder before it edits.

Each file ships as a skeleton with prompts in `<!-- comments -->`. Replace the prompts with your
own words; delete anything that doesn't apply. A file that's still just the empty skeleton is
ignored, so there's no harm in leaving one for later.
